The Release Order

The complete 1977–2025 saga experience.
Curated to minimize filler while preserving narrative integrity.

Curated by Lohith

Year / ID Title / Episodes Significance / Focus
1977 A New Hope (Ep IV) The introduction to The Force and the Jedi.
1980 The Empire Strikes Back (Ep V) Introduces Yoda and the greatest plot twist in cinema.
1983 Return of the Jedi (Ep VI) The redemption of Anakin Skywalker.
1999 The Phantom Menace (Ep I) Origins of Anakin & Obi-Wan. Duel of the Fates.
2002 Attack of the Clones (Ep II) Begins the Clone Wars.
2005 Revenge of the Sith (Ep III) The rise of the Empire. Order 66.
2008 The Clone Wars (Movie) Ahsoka's introduction
2008 Clone Wars (Season 1) Eps 2–5 Malevolence, Domino Squad (Echo/Fives).
2009 Clone Wars (Season 2) Eps 5–8, 10, 12–14, 20–22 Geonosis, The Deserter, Mandalore Plot, Boba Fett.
2010 Clone Wars (Season 3) Eps 1–2, 12–14, 15–20 Clone Cadets, Nightsisters, Mortis, Citadel.
2011 Clone Wars (Season 4) Eps 7–10, 19–22 Umbara (Rex's Trauma), Maul's Return.
2012 Clone Wars (Season 5) Eps 1–9, 14–20 Onderon (Saw Gerrera), Maul takes Mandalore, Ahsoka leaves.
2014 Clone Wars (Season 6) Eps 1–4, 10–13 Fives/Inhibitor Chips, Mystery of Sifo-Dyas, Yoda.
2014 Star Wars Rebels (All Seasons) Watch All. Crucial lore. Introduces Thrawn.
2016 Rogue One Direct prequel to A New Hope. Widely considered the best modern movie.
2018 Solo Han Solo origin story. Fun adventure, low stakes.
2019 Jedi: Fallen Order Canon Video Game. Intro of Cal Kestis & Inquisitors.
2019 The Mandalorian (S1 & S2) Introduces Grogu.
2020 Clone Wars (Season 7) Eps 1–4, 9–12 The Bad Batch Intro & The Siege of Mandalore.
2021 The Bad Batch (S1) Immediate aftermath of Order 66.
2021 Book of Boba Fett (Required for Mando S3)
2022 Obi-Wan Kenobi Vader vs Obi-Wan rematch.
2022 Andor (Season 1) Masterpiece. Gritty Rebellion origin.
2022 Tales of the Jedi Backstory for Dooku and Ahsoka.
2023 The Mandalorian (S3) The retaking of Mandalore.
2023 Jedi: Survivor Canon Video Game. Sequel to Fallen Order.
2023 Ahsoka (Season 1) Sequel to Rebels. Introduction of Baylan Skoll.
2024 The Bad Batch (S2 & S3) Conclusion of the Clone storyline.
2024 Tales of the Empire Morgan Elsbeth & Barriss Offee origins.
2024 Skeleton Crew Jude Law. Amblin-style adventure in the New Republic era.
2025 Andor (Season 2) The Final Link. Leads directly into Rogue One.
